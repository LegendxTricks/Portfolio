Paper 2 — CSAT Analysis
Qualifying paper — minimum 40% needed
✅ Good News: Paper 2 is qualifying only. Score does NOT count for merit. Just need 80/200 marks (40%). No negative marking. MPPSC CSAT is significantly easier than UPSC CSAT.
SectionApprox QuestionsDifficultyKey Strategy
Reading Comprehension (Hindi passages)15-20EasyRead passage carefully, answers directly from text
Reading Comprehension (English passages)5-10EasyBasic comprehension, no inference
Basic Numeracy & Arithmetic15-20ModerateSpeed + accuracy — Practice percentage, ratio
Data Interpretation (Bar/Pie charts)5-10EasyRead chart carefully, simple calculations
Logical Reasoning (Series, Coding)10-15ModerateNumber/Letter series, direction sense
Mental Ability (Syllogism, Decision Making)10-15EasyUse elimination, focus on decision making ethics Qs
Communication Theory (2023-25 NEW)10-15Easy7Cs of communication, types, Kinesics, Proxemics
Hindi Grammar & Language15-20ModerateVyakaran — Sandhi, Samas, Viloym, Paryayvachi
CSAT Minimum Strategy (2-week preparation)
Step 1: Do ALL Reading Comprehension questions — these are guaranteed 30-40 marks.
Step 2: Practice basic arithmetic (% , ratio, time-speed) — 15-20 marks.
Step 3: Learn 7Cs of Communication + Kinesics/Proxemics — new trend, easy 15-20 marks.
Step 4: Hindi grammar basics — 10-15 marks.
Total targetable: 80-90 marks with 2 weeks focused prep.

3-Month Week-by-Week Study Plan
Based on PYQ frequency, weightage, and difficulty analysis
📅 This plan assumes ~6-8 hours daily study. Adjust as per your baseline. PYQs should be solved at the END of each topic for real exam feeling. Daily revision of previous day's notes for 30 min is mandatory.
MONTH 1 — FOUNDATION (Static Subjects)
Week 1
Polity Foundation + MP Polity
  • Indian Constitution: Preamble, Parts 1-4 (FR, DPSP, FD)
  • Important Articles: 12-35 (FRs), 36-51 (DPSP), 51A (FD), 338, 342, 332, 334
  • Constitutional Amendments: 42nd, 44th, 73rd, 74th, 86th, 101st — exact provisions
  • Basic Structure Doctrine — Kesavananda Bharati case
  • MP Governor history: Complete list, tenure data, President Rule dates
  • SC/ST Atrocities Act 1989 — all key sections
  • Solve 2017 + 2019 Polity PYQs
Week 2
History — Ancient + Medieval
  • Vedic Age: Rigvedic → Later Vedic → Upanishads | Brahmana texts mapped to each Veda
  • Mauryan Empire: Ashoka's Dhamma, Edicts, administration, decline
  • Gupta Age: Science, art, literature highlights
  • Temple Architecture: Nagara, Dravida, Vesara styles with examples
  • Delhi Sultanate: All 5 dynasties — founders, key rulers, important policies
  • Bhakti/Sufi movement: Key saints, their locations, philosophies
  • MP Medieval Dynasties: Paramara (Bhoj), Chandela (Dhangdev, Mahoba), Kalchuri
  • Solve 2017 + 2018 History PYQs
Week 3
History — Modern + Freedom Movement
  • British expansion: EIC, Plassey, Buxar, Subsidiary Alliance, Doctrine of Lapse
  • British Administration: Regulating Act → Pitt's India Act → Charter Acts → 1909/1919/1935 Acts
  • Revolt of 1857: Causes, leaders, centres — ESPECIALLY MP centres
  • INC: Founding, Moderates vs Extremists, Sessions in MP
  • Gandhi Era: NCM (1920), CDM (1930 — Dandi March 6 April), Quit India (1942)
  • Social Reform: Ram Mohan Roy, Arya Samaj, Ramakrishna Mission, etc.
  • MP 1857: Shankar Shah, Raghunath Shah, Ranmat Singh, Tantya Bhil, Turiya Satyagraha
  • Freedom Fighters of MP: Chandrashekhar Azad (born Jhabua), Bhawani Prasad Mishra
  • Solve 2019 + 2020 History PYQs
Week 4
Geography — India + Physical + MP
  • Physical divisions of India: Himalayas, Northern Plains, Peninsular Plateau
  • Important mountain passes: Nathu La (Sikkim!), Lipulekh, Rohtang, Palghat
  • Indian rivers: Peninsular vs Himalayan, watersheds, longest rivers
  • Soils of India: Alluvial, Black/Regur, Red, Laterite, Arid — characteristics
  • MP Physical Geography: Narmada, Chambal, Betwa, Kshipra, Tapti, Wainganga — origin & towns
  • MP Districts: Minerals, industries, agriculture — complete mapping
  • MP Forest Cover: ISFR data, districts below 10% cover (Morena)
  • MP Tribes: Gond, Bhil, Baiga, Bharia, Korku, Saharia — PVTG list
  • Solve 2020 + 2021 Geography PYQs
MONTH 2 — DEVELOPMENT (Dynamic Subjects)
Week 5
Economy — Static + MP Economy
  • Basic concepts: GDP, GNP, NNP, per capita income, inflation types
  • Planning: Five Year Plans timeline, NITI Aayog vs Planning Commission
  • GST: Structure, slabs, Vijay Kelkar committee, GST Council
  • Monetary Policy: RBI tools (CRR, SLR, Repo rate), primary objectives
  • International trade: WTO, India's trade deficit, service exports
  • MP Economy: GSDP, per capita income growth, major industries
  • MP Agriculture: Soybean (MP's cash crop), wheat, dal, cotton production
  • India Agricultural Rankings: Who is largest producer of what
  • MP Schemes: Ladli Behna, Ahar Anudan, One District One Product
  • Solve Economy PYQs (2021, 2023, 2024)
Week 6
Science & Technology (IT focus)
  • Human Body: All organ systems mapped — kidneys (excretion), retina (image), Stapedius (smallest muscle)
  • Computer Networks: LAN/WAN/MAN, Ethernet=LAN, Modem, protocols
  • Cybersecurity: All malware types (Trojan, Ransomware, Spyware, Keylogger, Worm)
  • E-Governance: Gartner model (Info→Interaction→Transaction→Transformation), NeGP, DigiLocker
  • AI/ML/FPGA: Definitions, AICRA, robotics standards
  • ISRO: All missions — Chandrayaan 1/2/3, Mangalyaan, Aditya-L1, PSLV/GSLV
  • Nuclear: All nuclear plants in India, capacity rankings, BARC/NPCIL
  • Health missions: Sickle Cell Mission (2047), RKSM (10-19 years), NIRTH-Jabalpur
  • Physics basics: Sound in solids fastest, convection in liquids/gases, optics
  • Solve Science PYQs (2017, 2019, 2021)
Week 7
Environment & Ecology + Climate
  • Ecosystem: Food chain/web, trophic levels, energy flow (10% rule)
  • Ecological Succession: Sere, climax community — definitions
  • Environmental Laws: EPA 1986, Water Act 1974, Forest Act 1927, WPA 1972, BD Act 2002/2023
  • International Conventions: CBD, CITES, Ramsar, Basel, Stockholm, Rotterdam, Paris Agreement
  • Biodiversity hotspots in India, IUCN categories
  • Climate Change: GHGs, IPCC, Paris Agreement targets, India's NDC
  • SDGs: All 17 goals, India SDG Index, MP SDG score
  • National Parks & Reserves: Tiger reserves in MP (Kanha, Bandhavgarh, Satpura, Panna)
  • MP Environment: Forest types, Satpura Biosphere, wetlands
  • Solve Environment PYQs (2021, 2023, 2024)
Week 8
MP Special Focus — Culture, Heritage, Art
  • MP Heritage Sites: Khajuraho (Chandela), Sanchi, Bhimbetka (Wakankar 1957-58), Mandu
  • Bagh Caves (Dhar): 9 caves, Cave 4 = Rang Mahal, Buddhist paintings
  • MP Literature: Keshavdas (Ramchandrika, Kavipriya, Ram Rasayan), Alhakhand (Jagnik)
  • MP Folk Art: Mansukha (Western Nimar), Tertali, Phulkari
  • MP Craft Centers: Uchehara = Bronze craft (Satna district)
  • MP Awards: Kishore Kumar Samman, Kabir Samman, Ikkis Pachisi
  • MP Tribal Personalities: Birsa Munda (Janjatiya Gaurav Divas 15 Nov), Tantya Mama
  • MP Sports: Roop Singh Stadium (Gwalior), major sportspersons from MP
  • MP Tourism: Sustainable tourism villages, ODOP products by district
  • Solve MP-specific PYQs from all years
MONTH 3 — CONSOLIDATION + CURRENT AFFAIRS
Week 9
Current Affairs — Last 18 Months
  • Sports: All major tournaments — winners, player of tournament, venues
  • International: PM Modi visits, India-specific treaties & summits
  • Science/Space: Latest ISRO missions, scientific achievements, Nobel Prizes
  • Awards: All Padma awards (who got what award), state-level awards
  • MP Current Affairs: New schemes, appointments, records, events
  • Environment: New protected areas, international environment days, new treaties
  • Economy: Union Budget highlights, RBI policy decisions, trade data
  • Health: New disease outbreaks, government health schemes launched
  • Read: Daily Current Affairs from Vision IAS / Drishti IAS for past 18 months
Week 10
Full Mock Tests + Gap Analysis
  • Take 2 full MPPSC mock tests (100Q, 2 hours each) under exam conditions
  • Analyze weak areas: Note which subjects have highest error rate
  • Re-study weak areas from previous weeks' notes
  • Solve 2023 + 2024 complete Paper 1 as mock tests
  • Focus on "trap questions": NOT type, pair mismatch type, statement I/II type
  • Practice speed: 100 questions in 120 minutes = 72 seconds per question
Weeks 11-12
Rapid Revision + High-Yield Topics
  • Revise all 25 Golden MCQ topics daily (1 hour)
  • Create flashcards for: all MP minerals, river-town pairs, constitutional articles
  • Revise all "★★★ MUST" topics from every subject
  • Solve 2017 full paper under exam conditions
  • Current affairs: Last-minute events from the week before exam
  • Final revision: 50-point last week checklist (below)
  • CSAT Paper 2: 2-3 hours total — comprehension + basic arithmetic practice
